See a lead you want to handle personally? Tag it and Echo goes quiet on that conversation instantly — no replies, no follow-ups, nothing. You take the thread. Done with it? Untag it and Echo picks right back up. You can do it from your phone in two seconds, standing on a roof.

Echo knows what it doesn't know. The second a lead asks something only you can answer — a question about their specific bid, a complaint, anything legal — Echo stops, hands it to you, and tells the homeowner you'll follow up. It will never make something up to fill the silence.
